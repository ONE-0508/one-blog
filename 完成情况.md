## 代码依据（抽样）

- 前台文章列表/详情：`frontend/src/pages/HomePage.tsx`、`frontend/src/pages/ArchivePage.tsx`、`frontend/src/pages/PostDetailPage.tsx`
- 文章后端 API：`backend/src/routes/article.routes.ts`、`backend/src/controllers/article.controller.ts`
- 管理端文章列表/编辑：`admin/src/pages/articles/list/index.tsx`、`admin/src/pages/articles/editor/index.tsx`
- 文章模型：`backend/src/models/article.model.ts`
- 认证与路由守卫：`backend/src/routes/auth.routes.ts`、`frontend/src/components/auth/ProtectedRoute.tsx`
